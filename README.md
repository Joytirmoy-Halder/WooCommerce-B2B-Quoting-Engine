## 👋 Welcome!

Have you ever wanted to hide the "Add to Cart" button for certain products and force users to ask for a custom quote instead? The **WooCommerce B2B Quoting Engine** does exactly that, but without slowing down your site or battling with your theme's design.

Whether you run a wholesale business, sell highly customizable machinery, or just need to negotiate prices before a customer buys, this plugin gives you a professional, frictionless pipeline to receive exactly what your customers want. 

---

## ✨ Why Choose This Plugin?

*   🛒 **Hassle-Free Quote Cart:** Your customers can browse your store and click "Add to Quote Request" just like they would normally shop. Their items are saved safely to a special Quote Cart!
*   🎈 **Floating Cart Widget:** A gorgeous, helpful little floating badge automatically follows your customers around the site, showing them exactly how many items they have in their quote. 
*   ⚡ **Zero Technical Headaches:** Have a heavily customized theme like *Elementor* or *WoodMart*? No problem. This plugin is brilliantly coded to visually hide original quantity buttons and standard cart features elegantly without you needing to write a single line of code.
*   🚀 **Lightweight & Fast:** Unlike bloated plugins that try to do a million things at once, this plugin is tailored specifically for store owners who want a clean, fast experience. It bypasses aggressive hosting caches dynamically, so your site stays exceptionally fast.
*   🗄️ **Clean Backend Dashboard:** Manage all of your submitted quotes from a beautiful, simple dashboard inside your WordPress admin. Read client notes, review what products they requested, change the lead status to "Accepted" or "Rejected", and keep private notes for yourself.

## 🛠️ How to Install

1. Download the plugin as a `.zip` file from this repository.
2. Log into your WordPress Dashboard.
3. Go to **Plugins > Add New** and click **Upload Plugin** at the top.
4. Choose the zip file and click **Install Now**.
5. Once installed, click **Activate**. 
6. *That's it!* The plugin will automatically create a new "Quote Request Cart" page on your website for you.

---

## ⚙️ Simple Settings Guide

Navigate to **WooCommerce > Settings** and click the new **B2B Quoting** tab at the top. Here is what you can do:

1. **The Master Switch:** Instantly turn your *entire store* into a Quote-Only system with one click, or leave it off to choose specific items.
2. **Target Specific Categories:** Only want to offer quotes on items in your "Heavy Equipment" category? Just select the category from the dropdown!
3. **Change the Colors:** Use the simple color picker box to match the Quote buttons and Cart messages exactly to your brand's primary colors.
